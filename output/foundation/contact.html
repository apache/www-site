Contact Us

Before sending mail to the address below, make sure that the information on the FAQ doesn't already address your issue. If it is, the only response you'll receive is a pointer to that page.

Project Contacts¶

All technical questions and questions about a specific ASF project should be addressed to the appropriate project-specific mailing list.

Security Contacts¶

Visit the ASF Security page to learn how to report a security vulnerability.

Academic Researchers¶

To ensure that our communities are respected, and to make it easier for researchers to find the right information, our volunteers maintain a Research Information Portal.
