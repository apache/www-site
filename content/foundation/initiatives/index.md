ASF Initiatives are specific Targeted Sponsorships that provide additional financial support for Foundation governance operations, open source projects, and project communities. 

The ASF Tooling Initiative, launched in March 2025, was created to meet the growing global demand for ASF software by hardening ubiquitous Apache projects that benefit the greater open source ecosystem. The [Responsible AI Initiative](/foundation/initiatives/responsibleai), launched in April 2026, is aimed at strengthening the open source technologies that underpin modern artificial intelligence systems.

To learn more and sponsor, email [fundraising@apache.org](mailto:fundraising@apache.org).
 

## Tooling Initiative 

The [Tooling Initiative](http://tooling.apache.org/) helps ASF’s open source project communities streamline development, automate repetitive tasks, reduce technical debt, and better collaborate. Additional Initiatives are in the planning stages including security capabilities that meet the EU's Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) and US’s CISA recommendations. New products being developed under the Tooling Initiative are separate from the tools developed by [ASF Infrastructure](https://infra.apache.org/).

The Initiative requires an increase to the ASF's annual budget by $500K per year to maintain the Foundation's commitment to the highest standards of software development and community support. To meet these financial goals, the ASF has launched a new fundraising campaign to fund its Tooling Initiative. We thank [Alpha-Omega](https://alpha-omega.dev/) for contributing funds to seed the initiative.

### Pledge Your Support

Organizations interested in supporting the ASF Tooling Initiative are invited to become Sponsors at the following levels:

* Gold - investment $50,000/year
* Platinum - investment $125,000/year

Sponsors of the ASF Tooling Initiative enjoy the same benefits of [ASF Targeted Sponsors](https://apache.org/foundation/sponsorship.html) at their corresponding level. Custom sponsorships exceeding the above thresholds are also available. As with all Targeted Sponsorships, the ASF requires a three-year commitment to ensure continuity of service.

## Responsible AI Initiative

The Apache Software Foundation launched a $10 million [Responsible AI Initiative](/foundation/initiatives/responsibleai) to strengthen the open source systems that power today’s artificial intelligence. While AI often gets the spotlight, it depends on a vast stack of underlying technologies — from data pipelines to machine learning frameworks — many of which are developed within the ASF. This initiative ensures those critical systems remain secure, transparent, and sustainably governed.

As AI adoption accelerates, the integrity of its foundation matters more than ever. By investing in the infrastructure, communities, and standards behind AI, The ASF is helping ensure these technologies are built and maintained in ways that prioritize reliability, accountability, and long-term public benefit. We thank [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/) and [Alpha-Omega](https://alpha-omega.dev/) for contributing funds to seed the initiative. 

### Pledge Your Support

Organizations interested in supporting the ASF Responsible AI Initiative are invited to become sponsors. 

This Initiative is open to any qualified organization wishing to contribute funds and/or services, with a minimum investment valued at $250,000 per year for a three-year minimum commitment. 

Sponsors of the ASF Responsible AI Initiative enjoy the same benefits of [ASF Targeted Sponsors](https://apache.org/foundation/sponsorship.html) at their corresponding level. Those wishing to provide access/credits for AI models only are welcome to become Targeted Sponsors (separate from the Responsible AI Initiative).
